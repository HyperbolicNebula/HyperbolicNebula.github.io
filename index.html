The Clothing Lab

What Is The Clothing Lab?

The Clothing Lab is a project by Genevieve Walsh that seeks to unravel the mass-produced pieces of shit that very often don't conform to gender-diverse, Disabled, and fat bodies. Frankly, your life is a work of art: I want to help you make more of it.
