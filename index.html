The Clothing Lab

What Is The Clothing Lab?

The Clothing Lab is a project that seeks to unravel mass-produced pieces that very often don't fit our needs. Clothes and fashion try to impose a new reality upon our bodies as opposed to conforming to their surfaces. Nevertheless, as part of the human right to health and well-being, clothing should in fact help us feel good about ourselves and our bodies. Frankly, your life is a work of art: I want to help you make more of it.

Far too many people suffer from the headache that includes:

- fitting rooms

- lack of clothes that fit

- clothes that are difficult to put on

- excessive shrinkage

- garment longevity
