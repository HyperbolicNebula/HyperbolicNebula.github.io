The Clothing Lab

What Is The Clothing Lab?

The Clothing Lab is a project that seeks to unravel mass-produced pieces that very often don't fit our needs. Clothes and fashion try to impose a new reality upon our bodies as opposed to conforming to their surfaces. Nevertheless, as part of the human right to health and well-being, clothing should in fact help us feel good about ourselves and our bodies. Frankly, your life is a work of art: I want to help you make more of it.

Far too many people suffer from the clothing headache that includes:
- finding clothing we like
- fitting rooms and crowds
- size, fit, and shape
- dressing difficulty
- excessive shrinkage
- garment longevity
- repair difficulty

As a matter of fact, my "research" in 2024---a Google Form filled out by 25 friends---shows that some of the largest issues with clothing is that they don't bring us joy and ultimately it's difficult to find something we truly like. The Clothing Lab specifically seeks to cater to those who have specific difficulties with clothing, including but not limited to people with eating disorders, fat people, Disabled people, and gender-diverse people.
